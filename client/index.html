Quick and Easy to Use

Accessibility and a user friendly experience is the key to quick and productive communication. No downloads or installations required. SmartChat is an entirely browser based application. Simply start up the internet browser of your choice, navigate to the site and you're ready to start chatting.

Fully Functional

SmartChat integrates with various utilities to make your chatting experience streamlined and functional. WolframAlpha, Google and real-time weather tracking opens up all new avenues of interaction.

Cross-Browser Compatible.

SmartChat aims to be as unrestrictive as possible. Chat within the comfort of your preferred browser with the ability to chat with your peers using different browsers. SmartChat supports, Internet Explorer, Mozilla Firefox, Google Chrome, Safari and Opera.

Sentence Autocomplete

SmartChat will understand the words that you type and can finish whole sentences for you.

  • Save time while having fun talking with your friends!

  • Instantly know the current weather of any city by typing {weather-cityname}, or even know the answer to a math formula using shortcuts.
